About Elite TT Leaderboards

What is this?

Elite Dangerous Time Trials are community-created competitive races within the game Elite: Dangerous where pilots race through a course in the fastest time possible — whether in a ship, SRV, fighter, or on foot. Races take place across the galaxy, from bustling systems near the bubble to remote deep-space destinations.

This site pulls live leaderboard data and presents it in a way that makes it easy to browse all active time trials, explore race results, and track your own progress across every event you've competed in.

Getting on the leaderboard

To have your times recorded and appear on the leaderboards, you must use EDCoPilot while flying your time trial runs. EDCoPilot is a companion app created by Razzafrag that tracks your run, submits your time, and powers the entire time trials feature.

Without EDCoPilot, your times will not be recorded — it is an essential part of the time trials ecosystem.

Thanks
